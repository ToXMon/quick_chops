Our Story: From Nigeria to Mississippi

Founded by a passionate home cook who grew up in Nigeria and emigrated to the US over 20 years ago, Quick Chops brings the authentic flavors of West African cuisine to your table.

Settling in Mississippi, our founder missed the smoky, party-style jollof rice that brings people together. Now, she's sharing that homemade goodness in convenient packages.

Every batch embodies the ethos of authentic, homemade Nigerian food – rich, smoky, and full of love.
